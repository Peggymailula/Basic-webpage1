Foreign Language:

Kiswahili


Kiswahili also known as Swahili is a language I would love to learn because it is indigenous to Africa. Many African countries lost their native languages due to colonization,however Swahili managed to survive thus making it that much more special.It's a national language in Kenya, Uganda and Tanzania, and an official language of the East African Community which comprises Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi and South Sudan. Its use is spreading to southern, western and northern Africa.
